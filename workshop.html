What You'll Learn

Ninety minutes.
Five things that actually move the needle.

  1. 01
    Identity > Scripts (The IDGAF Frame)

    Buyers don't buy logic. They buy energy. The Sales Identity Framework: Belief, Conviction, Control, Calm. How to stop performing and actually become the version of you who closes, so the close starts feeling automatic.

  2. 02
    The Savage Mindset + FU Confidence

    The 5 core beliefs the top closers actually hold. Why comfort is the biggest scam ever sold and what FU confidence actually is (it isn't arrogance). The mental ground every close gets built on.

  3. 03
    The $2M Closes Framework

    A discovery that converts. Three areas, every single call: current state, emotional cost, future state. Plus the cost of inaction. Do this properly and the close is almost automatic. Objections come from a weak discovery, not from the pitch.

  4. 04
    Objections = Identity, Not Logic

    95% of objections are one of three things in disguise: fear, money, partner. How to handle each one with Validate, Reframe, Anchor, Lead. The person who's less emotional wins. Sales is a transfer of energy.

  5. 05
    Get The Role Without Begging

    The Western Method for landing dream roles. Identity shift, build the proof, hunt the opportunity, close the role. Why the people getting hired aren't the most trained, they're the most positioned. Stop applying and start being chosen.

The Opportunity

The infrastructure already exists.
You just have to be the person who closes.

Not dropshipping, not SMMA, not building a brand from scratch. Every other online model has a long unpaid runway. Sales doesn't. Companies selling high-ticket offers need people who can close. You show up, have the conversation, close the deal, get paid a percentage.

Roles pay anywhere from 8% to 50% commission. Students closing $5-20k offers from a laptop in Bali, Bangkok, Lisbon, and Melbourne. Some of them have been in for under three months. The skill doesn't have an expiry date.

Algorithms shift. Platforms die. Business models that looked bulletproof three years ago are saturated now. Sales just keeps working. As long as there are products in the world, someone has to be the person who closes them. You can be that person.
