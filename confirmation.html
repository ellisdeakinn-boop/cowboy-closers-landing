The Call

What actually

happens in the room.

No pitch decks, no pressure tactics. 30 minutes, straight conversation.

01
Where you're at right now

The coach asks about your situation — what you're doing, what you've tried, what's been in the way. No fluff, no warm-up.

02
Whether this is the right fit

They'll be straight with you. If this isn't the right move for your situation, they'll say so. No hard close, no manufactured urgency.

03
What the path forward looks like

If it's a fit — they walk you through exactly what's involved, what the timeline looks like, and what happens next. Questions welcome.

04
A decision

Yes or no. No "I'll think about it and follow up in three weeks." The call is designed to get you to a clear answer — whatever that answer is.
