Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 3, 2025

Cowboy Closers ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website and submit an application.

Information We Collect

When you submit an application form on our site, we may collect:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Information about your professional background and goals

We may also collect standard web analytics data (pages visited, device type, browser) through third-party tools.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Review your application and determine program fit
  • Contact you to schedule a discovery call
  • Send you relevant information about Cowboy Closers via email or SMS, if you have opted in
  • Improve our website and services

SMS / Text Message Communications

If you opt in to receive SMS messages from Cowboy Closers, you agree to receive text messages related to your application and booking. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies.

You can opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any message we send. Reply HELP for assistance. Opting out of SMS does not affect other communications.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data with trusted service providers (such as our CRM, email platform, and form tools) who assist in operating our business, subject to confidentiality agreements. We will not share your information with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes described in this policy, or as required by law. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Cookies and Analytics

Our website may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to analyse traffic and improve your experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some site functionality may be affected.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information at any time. To exercise these rights, contact us at the address below.

Contact

support@cowboyclosers.com

Changes to This Policy
