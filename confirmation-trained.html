Step 04
Go through your pre-call checklist.

These three things separate the people who get something out of the call from the ones who don't. Don't skip them.

Be honest about what's actually in the way

Not the surface reasons. The real ones. The call will be direct — the more honest you come in, the more useful it is for both sides. Think about it before you get on.

Know your numbers

What you're making now, what you want to be making, and what your actual timeline looks like. Vague answers lead to vague outcomes. Come in with specifics.

Show up on time, somewhere quiet

45 minutes. Camera on. No distractions. You booked it because you want something to change — treat the call like it matters, because it does.
